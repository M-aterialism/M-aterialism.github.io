Neural ODEs for Irregular Time Series

Your Name, J. Smith, M. Zhang
ICLR 2025 (oral)
arXiv:2501.12345
Abstract
We propose a continuous-depth framework to model irregularly sampled time series, achieving state-of-the-art on three healthcare benchmarks. The ordinary differential equation (ODE) approach naturally handles arbitrary time gaps and provides a latent trajectory that can be queried at any timestamp.
